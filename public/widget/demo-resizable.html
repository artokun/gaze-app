Gaze Tracker Widget

This widget displays an animated face that follows your cursor or responds to device orientation. The face smoothly interpolates between 900 pre-rendered frames organized in a 30x30 grid.

Try it out

  • Drag the corner to resize the container
  • Move your mouse to control the gaze
  • Click the fullscreen button (top-right)
  • On mobile: use two-finger pan or gyroscope

Usage

<gaze-tracker src="./path/to/sprites/"></gaze-tracker>
